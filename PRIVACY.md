# Privacy Policy for Volume Booster

**Author: Rama Jha**

Volume Booster is a 100% private and transparent browser extension. 

### 1. No Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or user behavior.

### 2. Permissions Justification
- **activeTab**: Used only to access the current page's audio content when the user clicks the extension.
- **scripting**: Used to inject the volume amplification logic into the active page.
- **storage**: Used strictly to remember your volume level for the current tab. This data remains on your local device.

### 3. Third Parties
No data is shared with third parties, and the extension does not include any analytics or tracking scripts.
